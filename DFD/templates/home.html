Deepfake Detection

Deepfakes are synthetic media where a person in an existing image or video is replaced with someone else's likeness. This technology can be misused to spread misinformation, damage reputations, and cause harm. Our mission is to empower users to identify deepfake content reliably.

Why Deepfake Detection Matters

Misinformation Control

Deepfakes can spread false information rapidly. Detecting them helps protect truth.

Privacy Protection

Prevent malicious use of your likeness by detecting manipulated videos early.

Cutting-edge AI

Our detection model uses state-of-the-art neural networks to analyze video authenticity.
